About Me

I am Jordan Vera, a freelance web developer whose main goal is to help businesses with their online presence by creating beautifully designed websites with optimization in mind from the beginning. I can help your company generate proper leads in only fractions of the cost of traditional marketing and advertising methods. My passion for web development began in 2016, and I believe that good web development is the key behind any good optimization efforts. All of my websites are created by me coding them, I do not utilize any content management systems (CMS) at all such as Wordpress or Squarespace. These CMS systems are great, but when taking optimization into consideration they aren’t the best. An <html> website often loads a lot faster than a Wordpress website. The reason many companies offer CMS developed websites is because they want you to be able to change the content within your website, but this can cause problems with on-page SEO efforts. Every single sentence on your website should be deliberately placed there for a reason. A saying within the industry is “content is king”, which means that our optimizations efforts should be based on providing valuable content to your consumers. Whether you need a static website, or a full fledge web application, contact me for a free consultation!
